Creating a more accessible
world, one hack at a time.

hACCESS is a 5-day, global online hackathon that fosters innovative solutions related to accessibility.


Taking place from June 14th to 18th!

Be a mentor or judge.

Imagine if we couldn't access certain spaces, images, videos, books, and more.

There would most likely be an uproar. Despite this, accessibility is often put on the back burner &, in certain cases, ignored until it serves as good PR for a company or public figure.


With technology and resources being more accessible than ever, we face a responsibility to give back to our communities. So, we call all innovators and anyone who has the desire to help those in need: get in teams of up to 5 and we will provide with some resources you may need to find & implement real-world solutions.
